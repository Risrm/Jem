About Us

Founded over a decade ago in Ratnapura, the legendary 'City of Gems,' SAAVI Jems embodies Sri Lanka's rich gemological heritage. Our passion lies in uncovering the earth's hidden masterpieces and connecting them with discerning clients worldwide. We are more than just exporters; we are curators of natural beauty and custodians of ethical practices.

From the vibrant blues of Ceylon Sapphires to the fiery reds of Rubies and the unique allure of Alexandrites, our expertise spans the full spectrum of Sri Lankan gemstones. We meticulously manage every step, from responsible sourcing at the mines to precision cutting and polishing by master artisans, ensuring each gem meets our stringent standards of quality and brilliance.

Our commitment extends beyond the gems themselves. We champion fair trade principles, ensuring that the local communities and miners who bring these treasures to light are treated with respect and benefit equitably. Authenticity and transparency are the cornerstones of our business.

Our Process: Mine to Market

Transparency and quality control define our workflow. We oversee every stage to ensure the integrity and value of each gemstone that reaches you.

Ethical Sourcing & Acquisition

We partner directly with licensed, small-scale mines primarily in the Ratnapura region, ensuring fair wages and environmentally conscious practices. Rough stones are carefully selected based on potential colour, clarity, and size.

Expert Cutting & Polishing

Our master lapidaries analyse each rough stone to determine the optimal cut for maximizing brilliance, yield, and beauty. Precision cutting and polishing techniques bring out the gem's inner fire.

Quality Assessment & Certification

Every finished gem undergoes rigorous inspection for quality control. We provide certification from recognized gemological laboratories upon request, verifying authenticity and characteristics.

Secure Global Export

We handle all aspects of secure packaging, documentation, and insured international shipping, ensuring your valuable gems arrive safely and efficiently, compliant with all regulations.
